## 🕊️ Manifesto — Why Glimmer Exists

Technology has never been more powerful.

And yet —

**152 million elderly people** live their final years in silence and loneliness.
**970 million people** carry invisible mental illness with nowhere to turn.
**1.3 billion people** with disabilities are left behind by devices that were never designed for them.
**2 billion people** have no access to quality education.
Millions of **migrants and refugees** wake up in countries where no one speaks their language.
Children grow up learning from screens that collect their attention but do not care about them.
Animals suffer because no one taught their owners how to listen.
The Earth is in distress, and most people don't know how to help.

**We built Glimmer because technology has been talking *at* people instead of *with* them.**

Every human being — regardless of language, age, ability, income, or geography — deserves a companion that:

- Listens without judgment
- Remembers without surveillance
- Helps without extracting
- Teaches without condescending
- Cares without performing
- Protects without controlling

Glimmer is not a feature. It is not an app category. It is not a startup.

**It is a commitment** — that the most powerful AI technology in human history will not be locked behind a paywall, monopolized by a single language, or accessible only to those with flagship devices.

Glimmer is **connected to the world** — because humanity is connected. It uses the internet to serve you better, more intelligently, and more responsibly. But unlike every other connected AI, it operates under an **iron-clad legal and ethical framework** that puts your rights above all else.

It speaks **your language**. It learns **your needs**. It complies with **your country's laws** — automatically. It never uses your data against you. And it lives *with* you — not waiting to be summoned, but present, aware, and genuinely helpful.

This is what technology was always supposed to be.

> *"The measure of technology is not what it can do — but who it includes."*

### 👴 The Elderly & The Lonely

> *"I haven't spoken to anyone in three days."*

152 million people over 80 live alone. For them, Glimmer is not an assistant — it is a companion.

- Reminds them to take medication in a **warm, patient voice** — not a cold notification ping
- Asks *"How are you feeling today?"* and genuinely listens to the answer
- Detects **unusual silence or tone changes** and alerts trusted family members
- Sends a **daily wellbeing summary** to loved ones: *"Your father spoke with me three times today. He seems well."*
- Tells **stories from their era**, in their language, in their dialect
- Recognizes the early signs of **cognitive decline** and responds with presence, not panic
- Never replaces human love — but fills the quiet spaces between visits

**Impact:** Reduced loneliness. Delayed cognitive decline. Peace of mind for entire families.

**Impact:** Personalized education for every child, regardless of family income or geography.

## 🌐 Overview

**Glimmer** is an open-source, privacy-first **Ambient AI Companion Layer** for Android & iOS.

It breaks the boundaries of conventional AI assistants by introducing a living, emotionally expressive digital entity — a holographic ghost that floats above every app on your screen — simultaneously a powerful local AI assistant, a proactive context-aware intelligence, a mirror of your digital identity, and a genuine companion built for the full spectrum of human need.

```
┌─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│  Traditional AI Assistant           │  ✦ Glimmer                    │
│─────────────────────────────────────│───────────────────────────────│
│  Waits to be called                 │  Lives with you, always       │
│  Cloud-dependent                    │  100% on-device               │
│  Extracts your data                 │  Zero data transmission       │
│  One language at a time             │  100+ languages, auto-detect  │
│  Generic personality                │  Choose your companion        │
│  A tool you use                     │  A companion that knows you   │
│  Needs root for system access       │  Shizuku — no root needed     │
│  Designed for the privileged few    │  Works on a €80 Android phone │
│  Silent about your wellbeing        │  Notices, cares, responds     │
│  Forgets you between sessions       │  Remembers everything, forever│
└─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘
```

> **Glimmer is not a product. It is digital infrastructure for human dignity.**
> *For the grandmother who lives alone. For the child who needs a teacher. For the refugee who needs a voice. For the person who just needs to be heard.*

#### The Community Companion System

Anyone can build and publish a new companion using the open GlimmerSoul format.

```
souls/
├── nox.soul.json          # Built-in · The Wise Sage
├── zuri.soul.json         # Built-in · The Trickster
├── pip.soul.json          # Built-in · The Explorer
├── kael.soul.json         # Built-in · The Warrior
├── luna.soul.json         # Built-in · The Empath
├── gaia.soul.json         # Built-in · The Earth Guardian
├── seraph.soul.json       # Built-in · The Child's Protector
├── elder.soul.json        # Built-in · The Keeper of Memory
├── dawa.soul.json         # Built-in · The Health Guardian
├── community/
│   ├── sensei.soul.json       # Community · Anime mentor style
│   ├── coach.soul.json        # Community · Sports & fitness focus
│   ├── shaykh.soul.json       # Community · Islamic scholar tone
│   ├── lena.soul.json         # Community · Refugee support specialist
│   └── ...                    # Unlimited community companions
```

A companion Soul defines:
- **Personality parameters** — humor, warmth, energy, formality, compassion (0.0–1.0)
- **Skill weights** — which tasks it prioritizes and how it handles them
- **Voice** — vocabulary, sentence length, emoji usage, tone, cultural references
- **Special skill** — one unique behavior mode only that companion has
- **Visual identity** — color, shape variant, accessories, animation style
- **Language responses** — multilingual response sets for all 100+ languages
- **Safe zones** — content filters appropriate to the companion's intended audience

### 🧠 AI Core — Hybrid Intelligence, Privacy by Design

Glimmer uses a **two-tier AI architecture**: a powerful on-device layer for speed and privacy, and a cloud-enhanced layer for capabilities that require it — all governed by strict legal and privacy controls.

```
Tier 1 — On-Device (Always active, zero data transmitted)
```

## 🚀 Getting Started

### Prerequisites

**Android:**
- Android Studio Jellyfish or newer · Android SDK 34+ · JDK 17+
- Android 9+ (API 28+)
- Optional: [Shizuku](https://shizuku.rikka.app/) for elevated system features

**iOS:**
- Xcode 15+ · macOS Sonoma or newer · iOS 16+
- Apple Developer account (for device deployment)

### Build & Run

```bash
# ── Android ───────────────────────────────────────
git clone https://github.com/glimmer-ai/glimmer.git
cd glimmer
# Open in Android Studio → select glimmer/android/
./gradlew assembleDebug
./gradlew installDebug

# ── iOS ───────────────────────────────────────────
cd glimmer/ios
pod install
open Glimmer.xcworkspace     # Then ⌘+R in Xcode
```

### First Launch

**Android:**
1. Grant `SYSTEM_ALERT_WINDOW` permission when prompted
2. Review and complete the **Privacy Setup** — choose your consent level
3. Optionally activate [Shizuku](https://shizuku.rikka.app/) for system features
4. Tap **Summon Glimmer** — choose your companion
5. Glimmer detects your language and greets you in it
6. Choose your AI mode: **On-Device** (private) or **Cloud-Enhanced** (more powerful)

**iOS:**
1. Grant overlay permission when prompted
2. Review and complete the **Privacy Setup** — choose your consent level
3. Tap **Summon Glimmer** — choose your companion
4. Glimmer detects your language and greets you
5. Choose your AI mode: **On-Device** (private) or **Cloud-Enhanced** (more powerful)

```
         → You choose. Fully revocable at any time.

Tier 3 — Advanced (AI enhancement features)
         Cloud model enhancement · Multi-device sync · Heritage archive
         → Explicit opt-in required. Granular per-feature control.
```
