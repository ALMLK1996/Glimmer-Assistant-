## 2. The Ten Privacy Principles (Our Constitutional Commitment)

These principles are not aspirational. They are binding commitments enforced in our code, our policies, and our legal structure.

| # | Principle | Binding Commitment |
|---|-----------|-------------------|
| 1 | **Human First** | Every data decision is evaluated against one question: does this serve the user? |
| 2 | **Privacy as a Right** | Privacy is the default. Sharing is the exception, always requiring explicit consent. |
| 3 | **Consent Before Collection** | We collect nothing without a clear, specific, freely given, informed reason. |
| 4 | **Data Minimization** | We collect the minimum data necessary. No more. We review this regularly. |
| 5 | **Full Transparency** | You always know exactly what is collected, why, where it goes, and how long it is kept. |
| 6 | **Right to Delete** | One request deletes everything. We verify completion within 30 days. |
| 7 | **Right to Export** | All your data is yours. Export it completely, in open format, at any time. |
| 8 | **No Data Sales** | Your personal data is never sold, rented, traded, or monetized. This is non-negotiable. |
| 9 | **Security First** | We apply the highest available security standards to protect everything you share with us. |
| 10 | **Legal Compliance** | We comply with the applicable law of your jurisdiction — automatically and without requiring you to ask. |

## 3. What Data We Collect

### 3.1 Data You Always Control (Tier 2 & 3 — Optional)

| Data Category | Purpose | Stored Where | Retention | Your Control |
|---------------|---------|-------------|-----------|-------------|
| Conversation history | Companion memory & personalization | On-device (encrypted) | Until you delete | Delete anytime |
| Voice recordings (if used) | Speech recognition | Processed on-device; not retained | Never stored | Opt-in per session |
| App usage patterns | Ambient intelligence | On-device only | 90 days rolling | Disable in Privacy Center |
| Mood & wellbeing signals | Wellbeing companion features | On-device only | 30 days rolling | Disable in Privacy Center |
| Language preference | Language auto-detection | On-device | Permanent (your setting) | Change anytime |
| Heritage recordings | Life story preservation | On-device (encrypted) | Until you delete | Full user control |

### 3.2 Data Required for Connected Features (Tier 1 — Service-Essential)

| Data Category | Purpose | Stored Where | Retention |
|---------------|---------|-------------|-----------|
| Account identifier (hashed) | Service authentication | Secure servers | Account lifetime |
| Device type & OS version | Compatibility & crash reporting | Servers (anonymized) | 90 days |
| General location (country/region) | Legal compliance framework | Not stored; used in-session only | Session only |
| Error & crash logs | Bug fixing & service improvement | Servers (anonymized) | 30 days |

### 3.3 Data We Never Collect

- ❌ Full name (unless you voluntarily provide it)
- ❌ Government ID or passport information
- ❌ Financial information of any kind
- ❌ Biometric data (face, fingerprint, iris)
- ❌ Precise GPS location
- ❌ Contacts list (without explicit permission for a specific feature)
- ❌ Photos or camera feed (without explicit permission for a specific feature)
- ❌ Messages from other apps (without explicit permission)
- ❌ Browsing history
- ❌ Children's data (users under 13/16 depending on jurisdiction receive enhanced protection)

## 5. How We Protect Your Data

### Technical Safeguards

| Protection | Standard Applied |
|------------|-----------------|
| Encryption at rest | AES-256 |
| Encryption in transit | TLS 1.3 |
| Key management | Android Keystore / iOS Secure Enclave |
| Network security | HTTP Strict Transport Security (HSTS) |
| Forward secrecy | Perfect Forward Secrecy (PFS) |
| Authentication | OAuth 2.1 + OpenID Connect + WebAuthn/Passkeys |
| API security | Rate limiting · Input validation · Output sanitization |

### Organizational Safeguards

- Annual independent security audit by a qualified third party
- Regular penetration testing
- Access controls: only authorized personnel can access servers
- Data breach notification within 72 hours (GDPR standard applied globally)
- Vulnerability disclosure program for security researchers

## 6. Your Rights

These rights apply to **all users globally**. Additional rights may apply based on your jurisdiction (see Section 7).

| Right | How to Exercise |
|-------|----------------|
| **Access** — Know what data we hold about you | Privacy Center → My Data → View All |
| **Correction** — Fix inaccurate data | Privacy Center → My Data → Edit |
| **Deletion** — Remove all your data | Privacy Center → Delete My Account → Confirm |
| **Export** — Download all your data in open format | Privacy Center → My Data → Export (JSON) |
| **Restriction** — Limit processing of your data | Privacy Center → Consent → Manage |
| **Object** — Opt out of any non-essential processing | Privacy Center → Consent → Opt Out |
| **Portability** — Transfer data to another service | Privacy Center → My Data → Export |
| **Withdrawal** — Revoke any consent at any time | Privacy Center → Consent → Revoke |
| **Human Review** — Request human review of automated decisions | Contact: privacy@glimmer.app |

We respond to all rights requests within **30 days**. Free of charge. No justification required.

## 8. Children's Privacy

We take children's privacy with the utmost seriousness.

- Users under **13** (or 16 in EU/UK): enhanced consent required from a parent or guardian
- **Seraph Soul** (children's companion): zero cloud data transmission, hardcoded at the system level
- No behavioral advertising directed at minors — ever
- No profiling of minors for any purpose
- Parental controls available for monitoring and managing a child's Glimmer account
- Compliant with: COPPA (US) · GDPR Article 8 (EU) · UK Children's Code · equivalent laws globally

## 9. Cooperation with Authorities

We take a principled, legally compliant approach to government requests.

**We will:**
- Respond to lawful requests accompanied by proper legal process
- Verify the legal validity of every request before any disclosure
- Provide only the minimum data legally required — nothing more
- Notify affected users whenever legally permissible
- Publish anonymized statistics in our Annual Transparency Report
- Challenge requests we believe to be unlawful, overbroad, or contrary to user rights

**We will never:**
- Provide "backdoor" access to any government or third party
- Comply with requests that lack proper legal authority
- Cooperate with requests designed to harm, oppress, or surveil users for illegitimate purposes
- Comply with requests prohibited by applicable law in our operating jurisdiction
