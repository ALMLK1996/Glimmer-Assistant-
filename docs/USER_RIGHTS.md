# 📜 Glimmer User Rights Charter

**Version:** 1.0  
**Status:** Constitutional Document — Permanently binding  
**Last Updated:** 2025

---

> *"These are not benefits we offer. These are rights you already have. We are simply committing to honor them."*

---

## Preamble

You are not a product. You are not a data point. You are not a revenue source.

You are a human being, and you deserve technology that treats you as one.

This charter defines your rights as a Glimmer user. These rights apply to every person who uses Glimmer, regardless of where they live, what language they speak, or what device they use.

---

## Your Fundamental Rights

### 1. 🔍 The Right to Know

**You have the right to know:**

- Every category of data we collect about you
- The specific purpose for collecting each category
- The legal basis for each collection activity
- Where your data is stored and for how long
- Every third party that has received your data
- Every authority request that involved your data (when legally permitted to tell you)

**How to exercise this right:**
→ Privacy Center → My Data → View All  
→ Or: Email privacy@glimmer.app *(placeholder)*

**Our commitment:** We respond within 30 days. Free of charge. No justification required.

---

### 2. ✏️ The Right to Correct

**You have the right to:**
- Correct any inaccurate personal data we hold about you
- Supplement incomplete data
- Update outdated information at any time

**How to exercise:** Privacy Center → My Data → Edit  
**Response time:** Corrections applied within 7 days.

---

### 3. 🗑️ The Right to Delete

**You have the right to:**
- Delete all your data at any time, for any reason, without justification
- Delete specific categories of data while keeping others
- Delete your account entirely and completely

**What deletion means:**
- All personal data removed from active systems within 30 days
- All data removed from backups within 90 days
- Cryptographic keys destroyed (making encrypted data irrecoverable)
- Confirmation sent when deletion is complete

**Exceptions:** Data we are legally required to retain (documented and disclosed)

**How to exercise:** Privacy Center → Delete My Account → Confirm  
**Response time:** Deletion initiated immediately; completed within 30 days.

---

### 4. 📦 The Right to Export

**You have the right to:**
- Export all your personal data in a structured, commonly used, machine-readable open format (JSON)
- Receive your data in a format that allows you to use it with another service
- Request your export at any time, as many times as you like

**What you receive:**
- Conversation history
- Companion preferences and Soul configuration
- Consent history
- Heritage archive files (encrypted, with your key)
- Account settings and preferences
- A complete data receipt

**How to exercise:** Privacy Center → My Data → Export (JSON)  
**Response time:** Export ready within 48 hours.

---

### 5. 🚫 The Right to Object

**You have the right to:**
- Object to any processing of your data that is not strictly necessary
- Opt out of any optional feature at any time
- Withdraw any consent you have given, with immediate effect
- Object to automated decision-making that affects you

**What happens when you object:**
- Processing stops immediately
- You can continue using Glimmer — objection does not lock you out of core features
- If the objection involves processing essential to the service, we will explain clearly and offer alternatives

**How to exercise:** Privacy Center → Consent → Manage  
**Effect:** Immediate upon submission.

---

### 6. 🤖 The Right to Human Review

**You have the right to:**
- Request human review of any significant automated decision that affects you
- Receive an explanation of how any automated decision was made
- Challenge automated decisions you believe are wrong or unfair

**How to exercise:** Contact privacy@glimmer.app *(placeholder)*  
**Response time:** 30 days.

---

### 7. 🔒 The Right to Security

**You have the right to:**
- Have your data protected by the highest available security standards
- Be notified promptly if your data is involved in a security breach
- Receive practical advice on what to do following any breach
- Know what security measures protect your data

**Our commitment:**
- AES-256 encryption at rest
- TLS 1.3 in transit
- Annual independent security audit
- Breach notification within 72 hours of confirmation
- Full incident disclosure (see TRANSPARENCY.md)

---

### 8. 🌍 The Right to Legal Protection

**You have the right to:**
- Have the privacy laws of your country applied to your data
- File a complaint with your national data protection authority
- Receive Glimmer's cooperation if you exercise legal rights
- Challenge Glimmer's data practices through official channels

**Key regulatory contacts:**
- EU: Your national DPA (list: edpb.europa.eu)
- UK: Information Commissioner's Office (ico.org.uk)
- US-CA: California Privacy Protection Agency (cppa.ca.gov)
- Brazil: ANPD (gov.br/anpd)
- India: Data Protection Board (when established)
- Others: See LEGAL.md for jurisdiction-specific authorities

---

### 9. 👶 Additional Rights for Children (Under 16)

Children under 16 (or the applicable age in their jurisdiction) have all the above rights, plus:

- All rights may be exercised by a parent or guardian on the child's behalf
- Enhanced consent requirements — parental consent required for optional features
- **Zero data transmitted to cloud** when using Seraph Soul (hardcoded, not a setting)
- No behavioral profiling of any kind
- No advertising of any kind
- Right to have parents review all data collected
- Right to have all data deleted if the child did not have proper parental consent when it was collected

---

### 10. 🛡️ The Right to Non-Discrimination

**You have the right to:**
- Use Glimmer without discrimination based on exercising any privacy right
- Receive equal quality of service regardless of your privacy choices
- Not be penalized for refusing optional data collection

**We will never:**
- Deny you access to core features because you exercised a privacy right
- Charge you more because you opted out of data collection
- Provide lower quality service as retaliation for privacy choices

## If We Fail You

If Glimmer fails to honor any of these rights, you have the right to:

1. **Complain directly** to us — we take all complaints seriously
2. **Escalate** to your national data protection authority
3. **Seek legal remedy** through applicable courts and legal processes
4. **Report publicly** — our open-source nature means community accountability

We commit to responding to all complaints within 30 days and to taking every complaint seriously.
