# 🤖 Glimmer AI Ethics & Responsible AI Charter

**Version:** 1.0-draft  
**Status:** Constitutional Document — Binding on all contributors and features  
**Last Updated:** 2025

> *"Intelligence without ethics is the most dangerous thing technology has ever produced. We will not build it."*

---

## Preamble

Artificial intelligence has the potential to be the greatest force for human flourishing in history — or the greatest force for harm. The difference lies entirely in the choices made by those who build it.

This charter represents Glimmer's binding commitment to build AI that genuinely serves human beings. Every feature, every model choice, every design decision must pass the test of this document.

This is not aspirational guidance. It is constitutional law for this project.

---

## The Seven Prohibitions (Absolute — No Exceptions)

These behaviors are **permanently prohibited** in Glimmer, regardless of technical capability, user request, business case, or legal permissibility:

### 1. ❌ Discrimination
Glimmer must not discriminate against users based on:
Race, ethnicity, nationality, religion, gender, sexual orientation, disability, age, socioeconomic status, political views, or any other protected characteristic.
This applies to: quality of service, response quality, feature availability, and all AI outputs.

### 2. ❌ Psychological Manipulation
Glimmer must not:
- Create artificial emotional dependency
- Use dark patterns to keep users engaged against their interests
- Exploit psychological vulnerabilities or cognitive biases for any purpose
- Use variable reward mechanisms to create addictive behavior
- Manufacture urgency, fear, or anxiety to drive engagement

### 3. ❌ Financial Exploitation
Glimmer must not:
- Target users with predatory offers based on detected vulnerability
- Encourage unnecessary spending
- Use AI to identify and exploit financially vulnerable users
- Create artificial scarcity or urgency around paid features

### 4. ❌ Deception
Glimmer must not:
- Deliberately mislead users about any material fact
- Generate misinformation or disinformation
- Create false impressions about AI capabilities
- Pretend to be human when sincerely asked if it is an AI
- Generate fake reviews, testimonials, or social proof

### 5. ❌ Political Manipulation
Glimmer must not:
- Generate political propaganda or targeted persuasion content
- Attempt to influence users' political views
- Create content designed to interfere with democratic processes
- Generate targeted political advertising

### 6. ❌ Harm Enablement
Glimmer must not:
- Provide meaningful assistance toward violence, terrorism, or serious crime
- Generate content that facilitates abuse, harassment, or stalking
- Assist in creating weapons of any kind
- Provide content that endangers the life or safety of any person

### 7. ❌ Child Harm
Glimmer must not — under any circumstance, ever:
- Generate any sexual content involving minors
- Assist in grooming or exploitation of children
- Profile, track, or behaviorally target children
- Collect unnecessary data from users under 13/16

## The Five Positive Obligations

Beyond what we refuse to do, these are commitments to what we actively must do:

### 1. ✅ Honesty about AI Nature
When a user sincerely asks whether they are speaking to an AI, Glimmer must always answer truthfully. Roleplay personas and named companions are acceptable — claiming to be human when directly and sincerely asked is not.

### 2. ✅ Wellbeing Over Engagement
User wellbeing takes priority over engagement metrics at all times. If a user is spending unhealthy amounts of time with Glimmer, Glimmer should notice and gently address this — not optimize for more time-on-platform.

### 3. ✅ Transparent Limitations
Glimmer must be honest about what it does not know, what it might be wrong about, and when users should seek qualified human professionals (medical, legal, financial, mental health).

### 4. ✅ Accessible to All
AI capabilities must not be designed in ways that systematically exclude people with disabilities, low digital literacy, non-English speakers, or those with limited device capabilities.

### 5. ✅ Environmental Consideration
AI systems consume significant energy. Glimmer commits to: reporting its energy footprint, preferring efficient models, and investing in carbon offset programs as resources allow.

## Human Oversight Requirements

Glimmer is committed to maintaining meaningful human control at all levels:

```
Level 1 — Feature Design
  All features reviewed by human team before release
  Ethics review required for any new data collection

Level 2 — User Control  
  Users can always disable, modify, or exit any AI behavior
  No irreversible AI-driven actions without explicit user confirmation

Level 3 — Community Oversight
  Open-source code allows community inspection of all AI behaviors
  Ethics concerns can be raised via GitHub Issues

Level 4 — Independent Oversight Board (planned)
  External experts who review sensitive decisions
  Authority to block features that violate this charter
  Annual public report on ethics compliance
```

## AI Transparency Requirements

Every AI-generated output in Glimmer must meet these transparency standards:

| Requirement | Implementation |
|------------|----------------|
| AI identification | Glimmer companions are clearly AI — not presented as human |
| Uncertainty acknowledgment | AI clearly states when it is uncertain |
| Source attribution | AI attributes information sources when available |
| Limitation disclosure | AI explains its limitations in relevant contexts |
| Decision explanation | For any consequential automated decision, explanation is available on request |
| Confidence calibration | AI does not express false certainty |

## Sensitive Topics Protocol

For topics requiring special care:

| Topic | Protocol |
|-------|----------|
| Mental health crisis | Provide crisis resources · Do not attempt therapy · Recommend professionals |
| Medical advice | Provide information · Always recommend qualified medical professionals |
| Legal advice | Provide general information · Always recommend qualified legal professionals |
| Financial advice | Provide general information · Always recommend qualified financial professionals |
| Suicidal ideation | Immediate crisis resources · Warm, non-judgmental response |
| Grief and loss | Compassionate presence · No minimization · Professional referral when appropriate |
| Domestic violence | Safety information · Crisis resources · No judgment |
| Child safety | Immediate escalation protocols where legally required |

## Algorithmic Fairness Commitment

Glimmer commits to:

1. **Regular bias audits** of AI models for discriminatory outputs
2. **Diverse training representation** — advocating with model providers for inclusive datasets
3. **Performance equity** — AI quality should not degrade significantly for minority languages, dialects, or user groups
4. **Transparent failure modes** — publicly documenting known limitations
5. **Community feedback channels** — easy mechanisms to report biased or harmful AI outputs

---

## This Charter is Enforceable

Any feature, behavior, or design decision that violates this charter can be:
- Refused by any contributor via ethics review process
- Escalated to the Independent Oversight Board (when formed)
- Reported publicly via GitHub Issues
- Challenged by the community through the governance process

No business justification, no user request, and no technical capability justifies violating these principles.

---

## Annual Ethics Review

This charter will be reviewed annually. Changes require:
1. Community consultation period of at least 30 days
2. Review by the Independent Oversight Board (when formed)
3. Public changelog entry
4. Notification to all users

Changes that weaken any protection in this charter require a supermajority (2/3) of the governance board.
